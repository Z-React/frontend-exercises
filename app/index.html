What to do?

Here is what you'll be doing for each task

  1. Read the instructions
  2. Modify the CSS, HTML and/or JavaScript
  3. In making changes, keep notes of your thought process, we will like to hear about it
  4. Save your work
  5. Share your work by pushing to Github (or by emailing us).
