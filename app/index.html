Thanks for applying!

We're excited that you're interested in joining the team! At Radius, you will be performing a wide variety of frontend and interaction tasks. To supplement your interview, we've created these tasks around Foodie Underground, a fake foodie blog about Austin cuisine!

What to do?

Here is what you'll be doing for each task

  1. Read the instructions
  2. Modify the CSS, HTML and/or JavaScript in folder tasks-yours-complete (find original copies of tasks in tasks-ours-incomplete).
  3. In making changes, consider keeping notes of your thought process as this will likely be discussed in future phases of the interview process.
  4. Save your work to the respective task folder within tasks-yours-complete.
  5. Share your work by pushing to Github (or by emailing your contact).

There are no wrong answers.

We understand in a real-world situation, there will be external factors impacting how things are marked up.
